# Security policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please do not open a public issue for a suspected vulnerability.

Email [support@permissiongranted.app](mailto:support@permissiongranted.app) with:

- a description of the issue and its likely impact;
- the affected route, component, or commit;
- reproducible steps or a minimal proof of concept;
- any suggested mitigation; and
- a safe way to contact you.

Do not access other people’s requests or data, send unsolicited email, degrade the service, or retain personal information while testing.

We will acknowledge a report as soon as reasonably possible, investigate it, and coordinate remediation and disclosure where appropriate. We do not currently operate a paid bug-bounty programme.

## Supported version

Security fixes are applied to the current `main` branch and the production deployment. Older commits and third-party forks are not supported.

## Public discussions

General hardening ideas that do not disclose an exploitable weakness may be raised through a normal issue or pull request.
